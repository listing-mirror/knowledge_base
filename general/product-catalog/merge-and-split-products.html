Merge & Split Products

Learn how to merge or split two or more product in Listing Mirror

Merging two or more products will combine them, displaying one as the parent, and the rest as the children. Splitting two or more products will separate them, creating individual products, or new products with similar children.

A product is defined as a Product Catalog in Listing Mirror, which contains all market listings for a given product.

Merge two or more products, creating one product

  1. From the Manage Listings page, search for the primary product
  2. Open the Product Catalog on the left.
  3. Click the Actions dropdown, follow by Merge Listings.
  4. Search for any SKU associated with the product you'd like to merge.
  5. Click Merge.

Merging listings once products are unified

Merging products does not mean you're merging your market listings. You'll need to merge them on the Product Catalog page, or the marketplace page.

Product Catalog

  1. From the Manage Listings page, click the newly created Product Catalog
  2. Using the Market Listing box, click Merge.
  3. Read the prompt, and click Merge.

Clicking Merge will delete your marketplace listings, and create them under the product variation.

Marketplace Page

If you'd like to merge your market listings without deleting them, you can merge them on the marketplace page. Learn more (coming soon)

Split two or more products, creating multiple new products

  1. From the Manage Listings page, search for the primary product
  2. Open the Product Catalog on the left.
  3. In the Actions box, click Split Listings.
  4. On the following page, use the right column to decide which products you'd like to split.
  5. Click Split Variations.

Splitting a product does not mean you're splitting market listings. You'll need to split them on the market listing page. Learn more (coming soon)

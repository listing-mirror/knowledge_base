Billing | Manage & Understand Your Subscription

Learn how Listing Mirror's billing works, and how to manage your subscription

We provide straightforward pricing, electing not to take a cut of your sales, or lock you into a contract. Our monthly or yearly subscriptions allow you to use the service at your leisure with no long-term commitment.

How our plans and your first 30 days will look

Our plans are based on the features Listing Mirror provides, packaged into what you need for your business. We want to make sure you're never paying for what you don't need. To ensure you're getting the most out of the software, you'll be provided a complimentary onboarding process during your first 30 days. If you decide the software isn't for you, we provide a 30-day money-back guarantee!

Do you have a question about our billing plans? Contact sales or, view our plans!

How to manage your subscription

Before getting started, ensure you're logged into the correct account. Verify you're on the correct account by going to your dashboard, and checking the Business ID at the top right of your screen.

To access the billing page, click your Business ID, followed by Billing Information.

Plan Level

The first thing you'll see is your plan level. If you're on the monthly plan, you can upgrade to the yearly plan to save. Regardless of plan, you can cancel at any time by clicking Cancel Subscription at the top right.

Billing Information & Invoice

Below your plan level, you can find your payment information, and invoices. The payment details input will be used to pay your current, or next payment.

To see all past and current payments, review your invoices. You can download a PDF of your invoice to view further details. If an invoice is missing a checkmark, the invoice has yet to be paid, and your syncing status may be disabled.

Agency

If you belong to an agency, and have multiple accounts, we will indicate which is the main billing account. Should you have permission, you can quickly switch to that account to view billing information. If not, you'll be denied access.

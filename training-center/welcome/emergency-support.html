If you need support from us outside business hours, we're happy to help.

Abusing this feature can result in your access being removed.


How to contact us during emergencies

  1. Open a support ticket.
  2. Set the priority to Urgent, and the subject line to EMERGENCY.
  3. After EMERGENCY, include a short summary.
  4. In the description, provide as much detail as possible.

Emergencies

  • ShipStation integration is disconnected from Listing Mirror.
  • All quantity levels are incorrect.
  • You're unable to update quantity levels.
  • You're unable to ship any orders.
  • Pricing issues are disrupting your business in a large percentage of listings.
  • You've been suspended from a marketplace due to an error.

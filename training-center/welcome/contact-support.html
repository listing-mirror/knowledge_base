Ticket Priority Level

High

  • ShipStation integration is disconnected from Listing Mirror.
  • Ten listings or more have incorrect inventory levels, and you're unable to update them.
  • Ten orders or more cannot be shipped, tracking cannot be added, or the marketplace isn't being notified.
  • Ten listings or more have an incorrect price, and you're unable to update them.
  • You've been suspended from a marketplace due to an error.

Medium

  • All issues that aren't a minor bug report or feature request, or fall under the above criteria.

Low

  • Minor bug reports, feature requests, and feature feedback.

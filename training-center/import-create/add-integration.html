Getting Started | Add & Manage Integrations

Learn how to add and manage your integrations on Listing Mirror

An integration is defined as any connection made to our platform. A list of integrations can be found on our website. Learn more

Add Integration

  1. From the menu bar, click Integrations, followed by Manage Integrations.
  2. On the top right, click Connect A New Integration.
  3. Search or select the desired integration.
  4. Follow all required steps.

We'll ask you a few questions about regarding how this connection will work on our platform.

If you don't see an advertised integration, or states, “Reach out to support”, or “Please upgrade your subscription”, reach out to support with the integration you'd like to add.

Manage Integration

You can view your integrations on the Manage Integrations page.

Enable & Disable

  1. From the menu bar, click Integrations, followed by Manage Integrations.
  2. Select your desired integration.
  3. On the General tab, enable or disable your integration, and save changes.

A disabled integration will stop all syncing to the marketplace. Aside from disabling manually, if we lose connection to an integration, it'll become disabled, and we'll email you as to why.

Delete Integration

Only a disabled integration can be deleted. Deleting an integration is not a valid troubleshooting step. If you're having issues with your integration, start by refreshing your credentials. Learn more

  1. From the menu bar, click Integrations, followed by Manage Integrations.
  2. Select your desired integration.
  3. On the General tab, disable your integration, and save changes.
  4. Back on the Manage Integrations page, click Delete.

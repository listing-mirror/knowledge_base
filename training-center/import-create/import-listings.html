Getting Started | Import Listings

Learn how Listing Mirror imports your listings after you connect your marketplace integration

When you add a marketplace for the first time, we'll import any listings you have on the marketplace, should they meet the import requirements. Once a listing is imported, we then use that data to build a Product Catalog. Learn more

Criteria for your listings to be imported

  • Amazon: Listings must be active, and have quantity.
  • BigCommerce: Listings must have a unique SKU, and not be a part of an existing variation in Listing Mirror.
  • eBay: Listings must have a unique SKU, and not be a part of an existing variation in Listing Mirror.
  • Etsy: Listings must have a unique SKU, and not be a part of an existing variation in Listing Mirror.
  • Google Shopping: Listings must have a unique SKU.
  • Newegg: Listings must be active, and have a unique SKU.
  • Reverb: Listings must have a unique SKU.
  • Shopify: Listings must have a unique SKU.
  • Walmart: Listings must have a unique product identifier, such as a UPC, ISBN, EAN, or GTIN
  • Wish: Listings must have a unique SKU.
  • WooCommerce: Listings must have a unique SKU, and not be a part of an existing variation in Listing Mirror.

We'll continue to import new listings from Amazon and WooCommerce every two hours. For other marketplaces, you'll be expected to create new listings on Listing Mirror.

If you have mismatched SKUs across each marketplace

When your SKUs match across each marketplace you're connecting, they'll be linked together under the same Product Catalog. This enables us to sync the inventory between each item on each market. Should your SKUs not match, you'll need to link your listings together to the corresponding product.

If your product variations imported as simple products

Rarely, a marketplace will break up your variations into separate products. Should this happen, you'll need to merge your product together, bringing the variations back together under one parent product.

Overstock

We're unable to import Overstock listings. You'll have to run a manual export on Overstock, and present that data to us.

  1. On Overstock, go to your Products tab.
  2. From the Export dropdown, click Full Catalog.
  3. Ensure Full Template and Horizontal.
  4. Contact support with your spreadsheet, and ask for Overstock listings to be imported.

Walmart & Newegg

Walmart and Newegg provide third-parties with incomplete data. During the import process, some information is not provided to us. Since we control your listing data, we combat potential data loss by making these listings ineligible for edits until you consent.

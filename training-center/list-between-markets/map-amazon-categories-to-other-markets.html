Map your Amazon categories to match other markets

Learn how to map Amazon categories to automatically assign categories on other markets

Once you've created or imported an Amazon listing, the assigned category will be mapped to the most appropriate category on another marketplace. You have the flexibility to modify these mappings as needed from the Category Mapping page. To get started, click Listings on the menu bar and choose your preferred region under Category Mapping.

These will apply to new listings. No existing listings will be changed.

Assign your default category mappings

If there's a category you want your listings assigned to, you can select a default for each marketplace. Any listings that doesn't have a specific mapping will be assigned this category.

Map Amazon-specific categories to market categories

You may want to change the mapping we've set up. You're provided with every category possibility you have on Amazon. Click Override, and select the desired category.

After you've wrapped up your category mapping, you can move on to posting your listings. Continued learning

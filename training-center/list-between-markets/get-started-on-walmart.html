Get started listing on Walmart

Learn how to use the data from your other market listings to create new Walmart listings, avoiding common pitfalls

Using your Product Catalog data, we build your Walmart listings with ease.

Before getting started, you'll have to ensure your barcode data, and Walmart integration settings are properly set up. Failing to do so can result in your listings not going live.

Common Pitfall: Missing UPC, GTIN, EAN, or ISBN

Walmart requires all listings to have a barcode, defined by Walmart as Product Identifiers. If your only other marketplace is Amazon, they don't allow barcode exports, aside from ASINs.

If you have a non-Amazon marketplace with barcodes, we'll import that data to your Product Catalog. Otherwise, you'll have to find the barcode elsewhere. You can find them on Amazon or Walmart.

With the gathered data, you can now input them into your Product Catalog.

Individual Listings

  1. From the Manage Listings page, search for your listing.
  2. Click the Product Catalog.
  3. Input the barcode into the corresponding field in the Identifiers area.

Multiple Listings

  1. From the menu bar, click Listings, followed by Bulk Edit Templates.
  2. From the dropdown, select Product Edit, followed by Download Template.
  3. Once downloaded, open the sheet and input appropriate data into any barcode cells.
  4. On the Actions sheet, switch UPC, GTIN, EAN, and ISBN from F to T.
  5. Save the sheet, head back to the Bulk Edit Templates, and click Upload Template.

List your products to Walmart

Create Individual Listings

  1. From the Manage Listings page, find a product missing from Walmart.
  2. Click the blue Add to Marketplace button.

If there are no issues, and you're syncing, the listing will go live.

Create Multiple Listings

  1. From the Manage Listings page, find a group of missing products on Walmart.
  2. On the left, click the checkbox for each listing you'd like to create.
  3. Using the dropdown, click Create.
  4. Click the Walmart checkbox, then hit Confirm.

Depending on the number of listings created, it may take up to a minute to display them all.

Create Multiple Listings Using Spreadsheets

  1. From the menu bar, click Listings, followed by Bulk Edit Templates.
  2. From the dropdown, select Create and Delete, followed by Download Template.
  3. Once downloaded, open the sheet and input C in the marketplace column and SKU row for the desired product.
  4. Save the sheet, head back to the Bulk Edit Templates, and click Upload Template.

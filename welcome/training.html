Request Help: Training Call

Learn how to request a guided training session with someone from our business

We understand that watching a video, or reading an article might not be enough. We offer multiple types of training opportunities, from onboarding, to software training, to everything in-between! From one person, to your entire staff, feel free to bring as many people as you need.


Exploratory Call

Unsure if Listing Mirror is right for your business? Schedule a call with someone from our sales team, and we'll dive into how we can help you succeed with us.

Start by opening a ticket with us.

Set the ticket priority to Normal, and the subject line to Exploratory Call

In the description, paste I'd like to schedule an Exploratory call with someone from your sales team.

One of our support staff will get with you shortly with a way to schedule the call!


Settings Check

Already a Listing Mirror customer? Schedule a settings check with our support team to go over your integration settings can be a great way to ensure your account is running efficiently. Please note, this is to go over your integration settings. This is not dedicated software training.

Start by opening a ticket with us.

Set the ticket priority to Normal, and the subject line to Settings Check

In the description, paste I'd like to schedule a settings check with someeone from your support team to go over my integration settings.

One of our support staff will get with you shortly with a way to schedule the call!

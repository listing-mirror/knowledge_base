If you need support from us outside business hours, we're happy to help. Please note, abusing this feature can result in your access being removed.


Emergency Support

Start by opening a ticket. Set the priority to Urgent, and the subject line to EMERGENCY, along with a summary of your issue. Be clear, concise, and provide as much detail as possible. From there, our staff will be alerted, and we'll address the issue as soon as possible.

Emergencies

  • ShipStation integration is disconnected from Listing Mirror.
  • All quantity levels are incorrect.
  • You're unable to update quantity levels.
  • You're unable to ship any orders.
  • Pricing issues are disrupting your business in a large percentage of listings.
  • You've been suspended from a marketplace due to an error.

Learn how to request a guided training session with someone from one of our specialized teams

We understand that watching a video, or reading an article might not be enough. We offer multiple types of training opportunities, such as, onboarding, software training, and everything in-between! From one person, to your entire staff, feel free to bring as many people as needed.


Exploratory Call

Unsure if Listing Mirror is right for you? Schedule a call with someone from our sales team, and we'll dive into how we can help.

Start by opening a ticket with us.

Set the ticket priority to Normal, and the subject line to Exploratory Call.

In the description, paste I'd like to schedule an Exploratory call with someone from your sales team.


Settings Check

Already have a Listing Mirror account? Schedule a settings check with our support team to go over your integration settings.

Please note, this is to go over your integration settings. This is not dedicated software training.

Start by opening a ticket with us.

Set the ticket priority to Normal, and the subject line to Settings Check.

In the description, paste I'd like to schedule a settings check with someone from your support team to go over my integration settings.


Individual Training

Would you like to know more about a specific feature? This is the perfect option for you!

Start by opening a ticket with us.

Set the ticket priority to Normal, and the subject line to Individual Training.

In the description, paste I'd like to schedule a call with someone from your support team to go over a specific feature in Listing Mirror. Then, provide the feature or topic you'd like to discuss.


Software Training

New or returning to Listing Mirror? Never had the time to learn the ins and outs? Hire new staff at your company? Schedule a call with someone from our success team to go over everything Listing Mirror has to offer!

Start by opening a ticket with us.

Set the ticket priority to Normal, and the subject line to Software Training.

In the description, paste I'd like to schedule a call with someone from your success team to discuss the features of Listing Mirror.

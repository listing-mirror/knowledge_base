Getting Started | General Orders

Learn how to use the general inventory features built into Listing Mirror

We provide features that allow you to control how orders are fulfilled, as well as edit the data provided by your customer.


Manual Orders

  1. From the menubar, click Orders, followed by Show All Orders.
  2. From the Actions dropdown, click Create Manual Order.
  3. Input all required information.

Once created, you can then fill the order yourself, or using 3PL inventory.


Shipping Override Profiles

Need to create a custom shipping profile for a marketplace? We can provide them for eBay, Walmart Canada, and Overstock.

  1. From the menubar, click Orders followed by Shipping Override Profiles.
  2. Click Create Shipping Profile.
  3. Input all required information.
  4. Click Create New Shipping Profile.
  5. From the Manage Listings page, search for a desired SKU.
  6. Click the Market Listing for eBay, Walmart Canada, or Overstock.
  7. Scroll down to Shipping Override Profile, or International Shipping Override Profile and assign your profile.

Depending on the market, there could be extra nuance. Our full knowledge base article goes over these in more detail. Learn more

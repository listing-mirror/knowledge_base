Getting Started | General Inventory

Learn how to use the general inventory features built into Listing Mirror

We provide features that allow you to control how inventory is sent to the market, as well as viewing inventory in unique ways.


Setting Maximum Quantity

You can limit how much inventory is sent to a marketplace for all your listings, or just a select few. Regardless of how much you have, we'll tell the market you have the amount you specify.

Individual Listings

  1. From the Manage Listings page, search for a SKU.
  2. Click the Market Listing for the desired marketplace.
  3. On the Listing tab, scroll to Max Quantity, and set the limit.

All Listings

  1. From the menu bar, click Integrations, followed by Manage Integrations.
  2. Click a marketplace, followed by the Inventory tab.
  3. Input the limit in the Max Quantity field.

Holdback Quantity

You can hold back how much inventory is sent to a marketplace for all your listings. Regardless of how much you have, we'll reserve the amount you specify.

  1. From the menu bar, click Integrations, followed by Manage Integrations.
  2. Click a marketplace, followed by the Inventory tab.
  3. Input the reserve in the Holdback Quantity field.

Inventory History

The Inventory History page will you show any time there's a quantity change for a SKU. This includes edits made by other users, orders, Amazon and Walmart syncs, Deliverr and SkuVault syncs, and more.

  1. From the menu bar, click Inventory, followed by Inventory History.
  2. Search for the desired SKU or title.

Etsy Shipping & Return Policy

Learn how to create your return & shipping profiles on Etsy for use in Listing Mirror

Etsy requires sellers to have a Shipping profile, and a  Return policy on their listings. Learn more

 

Shipping profiles can be used to get your packages to your customers faster. You can add upgrades to your listings, and offer faster shipping time for your customers, which also applies to Multi-Channel Fulfillment orders. Learn more

Creating Shipping Profiles

  1. On Etsy, open your  Shop Manager .
  2. Click  Settings, followed by  Shipping Settings .
  3. Click  Create Profile, and fill out the required information.

Once you're done, Etsy will send these to Listing Mirror, where you can set up your profiles.

 

Set Default Profiles

  1. From the menu bar, click  Integrations, followed by  Manage Integrations .
  2. Click any  Etsy channel, followed by the  Advanced tab.
  3. Under  Default Shipping Template, select a profile.

This will only affect future listings, and won't change existing ones.

Set Individual Profiles

  1. From the Manage Listings page, search for your listing.
  2. Click the Etsy Market Listing for the desired listing.
  3. On the Listings tab, locate the Shipping Template ID field, and select your profile.

For Calculated Shipping Profiles, Etsy may require you to add addition information to your listing, such as shipping weight and dimensions.

 

 

Return policies convey to your customers what they can expect for your return policy. Some items may be unfit for return, some might be economically unsound for return, and some return criteria may need to be specifically set. Learn more

  2. Click  Settings, followed by  Policy Settings .

Once you're done, Etsy will send these to Listing Mirror, where you can set up your policies.

 

Set Default Policies

  2. Click any  Etsy channel, followed by the  Advanced tab.
  3. Under  Default Return Policy, select a policy.

This will only affect future listings, and won't change existing ones.

Set Individual Policies

  1. From the Manage Listings page, search for your listing.
  2. Click the Etsy Market Listing for the desired listing.
  3. On the Listings tab, locate the Return Policy ID field, and select your policy.

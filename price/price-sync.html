Getting Started | Price Sync Options

Learn how to manage the prices of your items, either individually, or in sync across each marketplace

There are options when it comes to managing your prices, whether it be in Listing Mirror, or on Amazon. Listing Mirror was designed as an Amazon-first platform, leaving some workflows intertwined with Amazon.


Product Catalog

If your products are in sync with your Product Catalog, you'll only need to update the price once to change each marketplace.

Amazon Integration(s)

  1. From the menu bar, click Integrations, followed by Manage Integrations.
  2. Select an Amazon marketplace.
  3. In the General tab, enable Price Sync To Market.
    • Ensure Update Price and Price Sync are disabled.
  4. In the Advanced tab, disable Price Sync Target.
  5. Clear all price fields from Amazon market listings.

If you have more than one Amazon integration, you'll need to repeat this for each.

Non-Amazon Integration(s)

  1. From the menu bar, click Integrations, followed by Manage Integrations.
  2. Select a non-Amazon marketplace.
  3. Disable Price Sync Target.

With proper settings, you can now update your prices.

Individual Listings

  1. From the Manage Listings page, select a Product Catalog.
  2. In the Price field, input your price.
  3. Click Save Changes.

Multiple Listings

  1. From the menu bar, click Listings, followed by Bulk Edit Templates.
  2. Using the dropdown, select Product Edit, followed by Download Template.
  3. In the spreadsheet, update the Price column for the desired products.
  4. Save changes, return to the Bulk Edit Templates page, and click Upload Template.

Amazon Seller Account

If you're more comfortable updating prices on Amazon, you can do so there. We import the new price every 15-minutes, then send it to the other markets.

Main Amazon Integration

At this time, you can only do this with one Amazon integration. If you have multiple, you'll need to select a main channel.

  1. From the menu bar, click Integrations, followed by Manage Integrations.
  2. Select an Amazon marketplace.
  3. In the General tab, enable Update Price and Price Sync are enabled.
    • Ensure Price Sync To Market is disabled.
  4. In the Advanced tab, disable Price Sync Target.

Secondary Amazon Integration(s)

  1. From the menu bar, click Integrations, followed by Manage Integrations.
  2. Select a secondary Amazon marketplace.
  3. In the General tab, enable Price Sync To Market is enabled.
    • Ensure Update Price and Price Sync are disabled.
  4. In the Advanced tab, enable Price Sync Target.

Non-Amazon Integration(s)

  1. From the menu bar, click Integrations, followed by Manage Integrations.
  2. Select a non-Amazon marketplace.
  3. In the General tab, enable Price Sync Target.

For any non-Amazon channel you choose to leave Price Sync Target> disabled, you'll need to update the prices on the >Market Listing> for each marketplace.


Market Listings

If you need to ensure none of the prices are in sync, you can update all your prices individually on the Market Listing.

Amazon Integration(s)

  1. From the menu bar, click Integrations, followed by Manage Integrations.
  2. Select an Amazon marketplace.
  3. In the General tab, enable Price Sync To Market.
    • Ensure Update Price and Price Sync are disabled.
  4. In the Advanced tab, disable Price Sync Target.

If you have more than one Amazon integration, you'll need to repeat this for each.

Non-Amazon Integration(s)

  1. From the menu bar, click Integrations, followed by Manage Integrations.
  2. Select a non-Amazon marketplace.
  3. Disable Price Sync Target.

With proper settings, you can now update your prices.

Individual Prices

  1. From the menu bar, click Pricing, followed by Manage Pricing.
  2. Search for the desired SKU, and input your price.

Multiple Listings

  1. From the menu bar, click Listings, followed by Bulk Edit Templates.
  2. From the dropdown, click Price Update, followed by Download Template.
  3. On the spreadsheet, update the price column for your desired SKU(s) on the desired marketplace(s).
  4. Save the changes to your sheet, return to the Bulk Edit Templates page, and click Upload Template.
